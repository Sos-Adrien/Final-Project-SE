FAQ
What about allergies and danger using
those homemade products?
We associate cosmetic/hygiene products with allergies and danger because the industry use a lot of toxic ingredients. All products available here are made with fully natural ingredients. No Paraben, Alcool, Acid or Hydroxybenzhylaminil. Furthermore, I have already tested all products on my family and friends, they are still alive so far.
Don't hesitate to text me if you want to double check any ingredients associated with allergies.
How do you fix the price?
For every products, I charge the price for the ingredients + 1€. Not sure it is a sustainable business model, this is exactly what I test with this MVP. I will probably use the money I earn for new ingredients purchase.
What if I don't like the products?
If you don't want to use the product I made for you I would be sad... And I would send your money back + pay you a coffee/beer so we can discuss what I should improve.
How will I pay and get my product?
After selecting your products you will be asked where you want to pick them up. After that you can use paypal or N26 to pay and I will contact you to confirm your order. When your products are ready, we meet in your selected place to get your products.
I have plenty of questions and ideas.
Text me right away I want to hear everything!
